## Personas
User - a person, who operates, or interacts with a product.
Owner - a person, who owns the project.

## US-01: Subscription to a team

**Requirement Status:** Active
**MoSCoW priority:** Must Have

As a user, 
I want to subscribe to a specific hockey team, 
so that I can get notifications about mathches with my favourite team.

### Notes and constraints

The client requested that this condition should be in MVP.
